3) Comparison Operators (Boolean)
    Greater than (>)
    Less than (<)
    Equal to (==)
    Greater or Equal (>=)
    Less or Equal (<=)
    Strict Equality (===)
    Not Equal to  (!=)
    Strict Not Equal to  (!==)
    


4) Logical Operators (Boolean)
    AND (&&)
    OR (||)
    NOT (!)

    AND 
      -> Gives true only when all conditions are true otherwise gives false

    OR
      -> Gives false only when all conditions are false otherwise gives true

    NOT 
      -> negation


5) Conditional Operator (Ternery Operator)
